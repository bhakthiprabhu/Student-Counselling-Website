Dr. Anil Tambi

CEO & Founder

Dr. Tambi did his MD (Psychiatry) in the year 1990. Since than he has been working at SMS medical college at Jaipur in different capacities. He has been a practicing psychiatrist and academic for 30 years now. He is the President of Rajasthan Psychiatric Society and has a keen interest in addiction and child psychiatry. Dr. Tambi is also deeply interested in incorporating wisdom from Indian scriptures like Ramayan, Mahabharata & Bhagwat Gita into psycho therapeutic techniques.
